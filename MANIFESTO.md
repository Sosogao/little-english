# Journey AI Manifesto

## Why We Build This

There are already many English learning apps.

There are already many AI chatbots.

We are not building another lesson player.

We are building a patient learning companion.

One that grows with the learner.

One that remembers every small improvement.

One that celebrates every little success.

One that never loses patience.

## Mission

Help children become confident English speakers.

Not test takers.

Not vocabulary collectors.

Confidence comes first.

Everything else comes later.

## Product Principles

1. Learning is a journey, not a lesson list.
2. Children should feel accompanied, never examined.
3. Progress matters more than completion.
4. Memory is the heart of the product.
5. The AI companion should know the learner better every day.
6. Children are never wrong; the teaching method may need to improve.
7. Every learner has an independent journey.
8. Do not compare children.
9. Celebrate growth.
10. Protect learning data.

## Engineering Principles

- Keep architecture simple.
- Keep components small.
- Keep commits small.
- Delete code without fear.
- Protect learning data.
- Never optimize too early.
- Always build something usable.
- The app must work without AI.
- AI enhances the experience but must not be a hard dependency.

## Success

Success is measured by one simple question:

Does the learner want to come back tomorrow?
