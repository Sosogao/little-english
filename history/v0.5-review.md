# Version 0.5 Family Beta Review

## Positive

- Children could understand the adventure-based structure more easily than a traditional lesson layout.
- Celebration, next-adventure guidance, and Home page progress made the daily flow feel more complete.
- The learner-specific journey and review state worked as expected in local-first usage.
- Voice playback improved the usefulness of vocabulary, conversation, useful sentences, story sentences, and shadowing.
- Edge voice quality felt more natural than browser-only speech and was closer to the intended companion experience.

## Problems

- The early curriculum was too short, so learners finished available content too quickly.
- Daily continuity was not strong enough at first; after finishing one adventure, users wanted to enter the next day immediately.
- Home page and journey navigation needed clearer signals for completed, current, and locked days.
- Browser speech felt too mechanical, which reduced the companion feeling for repeated listening.
- Provider setup could fail silently during testing, which made voice debugging harder than it should be.

## Improvement Ideas

- Expand the curriculum into a fuller 30-day journey with clearer daily progression.
- Make the product feel more like an adventure companion and less like separate English exercises.
- Improve journey queue visibility so users can immediately understand where they are and what comes next.
- Keep voice provider architecture flexible while improving default voice quality.
- Continue prioritizing local-first stability and simple flows before adding more advanced AI behavior.

## Priority

### P0

- Curriculum depth: provide enough structured content for sustained daily use.
- Better day-to-day continuation: finishing one adventure should make the next step obvious and immediate.
- Better default voice experience: natural playback matters for repeated listening and child engagement.

### P1

- Stronger journey visualization across completed, current, and upcoming days.
- More explicit playback feedback and clearer provider error handling.
- Better review and celebration polish after the core curriculum experience is stable.
